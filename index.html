Mayuko stressed the importance of peer reviewing, another activity that will play an integral role in our course. product focus
The syllabus outlines the basic work expectations that will earn a specific letter grade and gives us details on how to assess our own work. We must go above and beyond if we wish to earn a grade above an 8, and Mayuko shares a related point: she accentuates the importance of working for an efficient and satisfying end product rather than focusing on a grade that we will receive. Even though this course is part of our GPA, it is more important to be working to create a high-quality finished product.
